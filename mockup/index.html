    About this archive

    The Owner's Box is a verified archive of federal political donations by the principal owners of all 30 Major League Baseball franchises. Every record traces back to a specific FEC filing. We classify each record CONFIRMED or PROBABLE based on a documented verification rubric. Records that match a donor name but no other signal stay UNCERTAIN and never enter the canonical dataset. This is factual infrastructure, not editorial analysis.
